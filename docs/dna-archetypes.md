# CoDNA AI: DNA Archetypes

The DNA Engine generates memorable, highly-shareable "Archetypes" that form the core identity of a user on CoDNA AI. Below are the official archetypes and the conditions required to trigger them.

## Core Archetypes

### The Algorithmist
**Focus:** A deep mastery of foundational algorithms.
**Trigger:** High ratio of DP and Greedy problems solved (e.g., DP >= 20% & Greedy >= 15%).

### The Graph Wizard
**Focus:** Dominance in traversal and network logic.
**Trigger:** Solves significantly more Graph/DFS/Flow problems than average (e.g., Graphs >= 20%).

### The Theorist
**Focus:** Heavy focus on mathematical proofs and logic.
**Trigger:** High volume of Math, Combinatorics, and Number Theory problems (e.g., Math >= 25%).

### The Marathon Grinder
**Focus:** Volume and persistence.
**Trigger:** Massive number of unique problems solved (e.g., > 2500 unique accepted submissions).

### The Speed Demon
**Focus:** Impeccable accuracy and speed during contests.
**Trigger:** Extremely high accuracy rate (e.g., > 70% first-try Accepted rate).

### The Specialist
**Focus:** Intricate data structures and advanced techniques.
**Trigger:** Disproportionate focus on DSU, Trees, SegTrees, and FFT (e.g., Structures >= 22%).

### The Competitor
**Focus:** Continuous contest participation and ranking grinds.
**Trigger:** High volume of contest participation (> 100 rated contests).

### The Problem Hunter
**Focus:** The default fallback for users who are balanced and don't strictly align with specialized domains yet.

---

## Modifiers & Traits

These provide additional color to the Archetype.

- **Veteran:** Highly experienced in competitive programming (> 200 contests).
- **Sniper:** High accuracy.
- **Brute-Force Debugger:** Low accuracy (heavy trial and error).
- **Giant Slayer:** Solves problems heavily outside their current rating band.
- **Explorer:** Solved few problems, still early in the journey.
